# Now — This Quarter's Focus

A single page that answers "what am I working on, and what am I doing this week?" Update weekly (or whenever the answer changes). Long enough to be specific, short enough to fit on one screen.

!!! tip "How to use this page"
    Edit it weekly — Sunday evening is the natural rhythm. The friction of updating it forces you to confront whether your stated focus matches your actual time use.
